This project will help you to hide an image in another image.
For Black And White:
copy file Black_&_white.m and main.jpg,hide.jpg (or you can replace the name with your files in the code).
For RGB Image:
copy file RGB.m and ironman.jpg,spiderman.jpg (or you can replace the name with your files in the code).
